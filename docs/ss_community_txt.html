We Value Our Guests

At Sam's Smoothie Shack, we always recognize our regular visitors! We love your support and look forward to seeing all faces, old and new. Therefore, we recognize our regular visitors based off of who has joined our email list and recommended us to friends.

Interested in being the recognized customer of next month? Share your interest in the business by posting a picture of you and your smoothie on Instagram or Facebook with the hashtag "samssmoothies". If you are on our email list and have posted your smoothie picture, you could be chosen in the drawing to win a $50 giftcard and have your picture posted on the website! Good luck. :)
